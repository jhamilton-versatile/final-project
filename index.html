My Top 10 Favorite Movies

As a kid, my favorite movies were from Disney. They helped me overcome anxiety from social interactions that I still sometimes struggle with. The stories and characters were relatable to me as I'm sure they are for you. Plus, who doesn't love musicals?! I grew up as a 90's kid so it should come as no surprise that most of these films are from the late 80's - early 90's eras.

  1. Mulan
  2. Aladdin
  3. Hercules
  4. Beauty and the Beast
  5. Pocahontas
  6. The Lion King
  7. Tarzan
  8. *The Great Mouse Detective
  9. The Hunchback of Notre Dame
  10. The Little Mermaid

*Note about The Great Mouse Detective: while not technically a part of this era, it should be noted that this was one of the films that prompted development for the rest of these films.
